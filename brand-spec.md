## 🎨 辅助资产 (Support Assets)

### 色板 (Palette)
- **Background**: `#050505` (Deep Black)
- **Text**: `#f5f5f5` (Off-white)
- **Primary Accent**: `#a855f7` (Purple)
- **Tricolore**:
    - Blue: `#3b82f6`
    - White: `#f5f5f5`
    - Red: `#ef4444`

### 字型 (Typography)
- **Display**: "Space Mono", "JetBrains Mono", monospace.
- **Body**: Same as Display.
- **Labels**: All caps for buttons and action points.

### 签名细节 (Signature Details)
- **Borders**: 3px solid, sharp corners.
- **Shadows**: Harsh offset shadows, no blur. `4px 4px 0px 0px var(--accent)`.
- **States**: 1px translate on hover, shadow removal.
- **Wording**: `HANDSHAKE`, `SCANNING`, `INITIALIZING`, `EXECUTE`.

### 禁区 (Forbidden)
- **NO** Rounded corners.
- **NO** Blur.
- **NO** Glitch effects (explicitly forbidden).
- **NO** Inter/Roboto/Sans fonts.

### 气质关键词 (Keywords)
- Industrial
- Robust
- Tactical
- Powerful
- Brutalist
